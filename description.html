Overview

The EduTools plugin empowers users around the world to learn and teach programming languages such as Kotlin, Java, Python, JavaScript, Rust, Scala, C/C++, Go, and PHP, through interactive courses right in their JetBrains IDEs. More than 700,000 users have already started learning or teaching programming with EduTools!


As a learner, you can enjoy a unique, interactive way to study programming while gaining experience with professional development tools. You can join a public programming course, work on JetBrains Academy real-world projects, complete CheckiO missions, participate in Codeforces contests, or enroll in a custom course created by your professor or co-worker.

Read the Learner Start Guide for more information.


As an educator, you can pass your programming knowledge on to your students, co-workers, or social media followers by creating your own courses and sharing them either privately or publicly. You can combine theoretical learning and hands-on practice in your courses, test user knowledge with different sets of tasks, and provide extra assistance with feedback and hints.

Read the Educator Start Guide for more information.
